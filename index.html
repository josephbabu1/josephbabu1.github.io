ABOUT ME

I'm Joseph Babu, a 2nd year Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Government Engineering College, Thrissur. Passionate about bridging hardware and software through embedded systems, digital signal processing, and FPGA development.

My engineering journey focuses on creating innovative solutions in communication systems, from chaos-driven codecs to real-time vehicle monitoring systems. I enjoy tackling complex challenges in digital design and signal processing.
